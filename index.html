About Us

Magic square academy is a learning experience that sets the standard for excellence in chess instruction. Integrating new conceps in teaching chess, our curriculum reflects children's interests and builds on their competencies, we excel in creating persolal bond with the students there by understanding there unique learning styles and tailor the training accordingly with personalized attention in every class.
